Supporting both HTC Vive and Oculus

The player who joins(creates) the game first, will be the driver.
The host can drive the tank:
	move forward and back by pressing the Axis2D button(right hand) up and down.
	to grab sticks using vr hands. Press grip button to grabb.
Second joining player will be gunner. He is no able to drive the tank.

The player can shoot:
	rotate the tower, move the tower up and down by pressing the Axis2D button(left hand) in appropriate directions. 
	use trigger button to shoot

To open setting menu press Primary button on the left hand. By using vr rays you can interact with the board. 
Press trigger button to select. Click "Driver" to come back to the box.
